**1. Hackathon & Prototype Nature**
TrustRoute is a Proof of Concept (PoC) software developed specifically for a hackathon environment. It is not intended for immediate production use, financial processing, or handling of real-world monetary assets without further security auditing.

**2. Simulated Fiat Integration**
For the purposes of this demonstration, the Razorpay Fiat-to-Crypto integration operates in a **Simulated / Bypassed Mode**. No actual credit card, banking, or real-world financial transactions are processed. The system mocks fiat finality to demonstrate the subsequent Smart Contract execution on the Corda blockchain.
